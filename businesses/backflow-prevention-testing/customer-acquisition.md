## Step 8 — Objection Handling

### Top 4 B2B Objections for Backflow Testing

**Objection 1:** "We already have someone for that."

**What it really means:** They have an existing relationship but may not be fully satisfied, or they're testing your persistence.

**Response:** "That's great that you have someone handling it. Quick question — are you completely happy with their scheduling flexibility and reporting speed? Most property managers tell me their current provider makes them work around the tester's schedule, especially during busy season. If that ever becomes a problem, we specialize in working around yours instead."

---

**Objection 2:** "Not the right time / budget isn't there right now."

**What it really means:** It's not urgent enough yet, or they don't see the value beyond basic compliance.

**Response:** "I totally understand. Most property managers don't think about backflow testing until the city sends a notice. Here's what I've found — the property managers who plan ahead get better pricing and avoid the spring rush. Would it make sense to at least get pricing now so you know what you're looking at for next year? Then you can budget accordingly and avoid the scramble."

---

**Objection 3:** "Send me some information." 

**What it really means:** Polite brush-off — they want to avoid a sales conversation.

**Response:** "I could send you a brochure, but honestly, every property management situation is different. What would be more valuable is if I could ask you 2-3 quick questions about your current testing process, then send you something actually relevant to your situation. Would that work better? It'll take 5 minutes max."

---

**Objection 4:** "How are you different from [competitor]?"

**What it really means:** They've shopped around or have experience with others in the market.

**Response:** "Good question — you've probably talked to [acknowledge competitor]. Here's the main difference: most backflow companies make you work around their schedule, especially during busy season. We do the opposite — we offer evening and weekend appointments because we know you have tenants and business operations to consider. Plus, we file compliance reports the same day instead of making you wait weeks. Does that sound like it would make a difference for [Company Name]?"
